About Us

Axiom is a startup competition where ambitious youth get the chance to pitch their insane startup ideas to a panel of experienced judges. Participants receive actionable feedback and compete for a cash prize that can help turn their vision into reality.

This event was created to give ambitious youth the opportunity to connect with experts across fields—especially tech, AI, entrepreneurship, software development, and startup leadership.

Before the pitches, there will be some for participants to connect directly with 15–20 mentors—professionals offering guidance and support.

There will also be plenty of opportunities to connect with like-minded peers (aka people who know unicorns really do exist in the business world).

After the winner is announced, there will be a small rooftop gathering to celebrate.

Attend:

Watch ten teams of ambitious entrepreneurs compete for a cash prize while enjoying free food and drinks. Attendees will also have the opportunity to connect with mentors and industry experts throughout the event. It's a double win! You can register now on our Luma.
