About

Axiom is a startup competition where youth aged 15-19 will get a chance to pitch their insane startup ideas to a panel of experienced judges, receive actionable feedback, and compete for a monetary prize that can transform their vision into reality. There will be opportunities to connect with like-minded people (aka people who know that unicorns exist in the business world too). After the winner is announced, we will have a small after party on the roof!
