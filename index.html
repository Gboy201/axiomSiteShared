Axiom

September 9 at Builders Club

Sofia Bodnar's Axiom Startups | Premier Startup Competition | Waterloo Tech Week | Founded by Sofia Bodnar

Axiom is a startup competition where ambitious youth get the chance to pitch their insane startup ideas to a panel of experienced judges. Participants receive feedback and compete for a 1k cash prize.

Before the pitches, youth will have time to connect directly with 15–20 mentors, professionals offering guidance and support. There'll also be lots of chances to vibe with like-minded peers, including a round of startup trivia :D

Axiom was created by a student for students to give ambitious youth the chance to connect with experts in tech, AI, entrepreneurship, and software development, and get insane opportunities.
